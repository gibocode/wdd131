Site Plan

Name

Think Pawsitive

  • Description: This is the name of the site. It is a play on words, combining "think" and "pawsitive". Pawsitive is the same as positive, but with a paw.
  • Domain: https://www.thinkpawsitive.com (non-existent)

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide positivity and solutions for dog owners and their dogs about food, training, and other dog related stuff.

Scenarios

  • What is the best food for my dog?
  • What is the best training method for my dog?
  • What is the best way to train my dog?
